RESEARCH / PROJECTS

DESIGN OF DUAL BAND MICROSTRIP PATCH ANTENNA FOR 5G APPLICATIONS.

Authors: Md. Omar Faruk Noman, Prof. Dr. Md. Azad Hossain

This study presents a Microstrip antenna with dual band properties, competent for 5G technology. Proposed antenna is rectangular in shaped with a U slot added to the patch and designed on 0.8mm thick FR-4 (lossy) substrate (ε = 4.3) that offers a loss factor of 0.02. The substrate has a dimension of 8mm x 8mm with height of 0.8mm. The proposed antenna resonates at 30.64 GHz and 38.84 GHz which lies within the spectrum for 5G technology. The S parameters of antenna is calculated as -36.8954 dB and -56.836325 dB at 30.64 GHz and 38.84 GHz respectively which is satisfactory enough. VSWR of the antenna is 1.0291 and 1.0033 which indicates the good matching of antenna impedance. The proposed antenna shows a bandwidth of 2.295 GHz and 3.45 GHz respectively at two frequency band. Gain is calculated as 4.57 dBi and 7.59 dBi respectively. Proposed antenna shows directional radiation pattern with very negligible spurious radiation. Because of being dual band, it’s able to operate in multiple frequency band thus requiring less volume and serving multiple purpose.
